Rishikesh

About Rishikesh

Rishikesh is the Yoga Capital of the World, while Haridwar is a major Hindu pilgrimage site. Both towns are located along the sacred Ganges River. Rishikesh is known for its ashrams, yoga schools, and adventure sports like white-water rafting.

Popular Spots

  • Lakshman Jhula & Ram Jhula
  • Triveni Ghat & Parmarth Niketan Ashram
  • Neelkanth Mahadev Temple
