Explore India's Top 10 Tourist Destinations

Discover the beauty and diversity of India

Taj Mahal, Agra

One of the Seven Wonders of the World, this white marble mausoleum is a symbol of eternal love.

Jaipur, Rajasthan

The Pink City is famous for its forts, palaces, and vibrant culture.

Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh

One of the world's oldest living cities, known for its spiritual experiences and Ganges River ghats.

Goa

Known for its pristine beaches, vibrant nightlife, and Portuguese heritage, Goa is a paradise for travelers.

Kerala

Experience serene backwaters in Alleppey, Munnar's tea gardens, and exotic wildlife in Periyar.

Ladakh

Famous for its breathtaking landscapes, Pangong Lake, Nubra Valley, and monasteries.

Rishikesh

Rishikesh is known as the "Yoga Capital of the World," while Haridwar is famous for the Ganga Aarti at Har Ki Pauri.

Andaman & Nicobar Islands

Stunning beaches, crystal-clear waters, and adventure activities like scuba diving at Havelock Island.

Mysore

Stunning beaches, crystal-clear waters, and adventure activities like scuba diving at Havelock Island.

Darjeeling

Famous for its tea plantations, the Darjeeling Himalayan Railway (Toy Train), and the breathtaking view of Kanchenjunga.
