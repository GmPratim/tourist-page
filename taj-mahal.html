Taj Mahal, Agra

About Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal is an ivory-white marble mausoleum in Agra, built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal. It is one of the Seven Wonders of the World and a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
