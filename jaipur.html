Jaipur, Rajasthan (The Pink City)

About Jaipur

Jaipur, the capital of Rajasthan, is known for its royal palaces, vibrant markets, and desert culture. The city is part of India’s Golden Triangle (Delhi-Agra-Jaipur), making it a must-visit destination.

Popular Spots

  • Amber Fort
  • Hawa Mahal
  • City Palace
